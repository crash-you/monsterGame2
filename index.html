Game Overview

Monster Survivors is a thrilling survival game where you face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience to level up. Choose upgrades wisely to enhance your survival chances as the difficulty increases with time.

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from various characters with unique abilities and play styles.

Upgrade System

Level up and enhance your character's abilities as you progress.

Endless Challenges

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival mode.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors draws inspiration from popular roguelike survival games, offering a unique twist with its monster-themed world. Your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible against endless waves of increasingly powerful monsters.

As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience points to level up and enhance your character's abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely, as the right combination of skills can make the difference between survival and defeat.

With its simple controls but deep strategic elements, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive in this monster-infested world?

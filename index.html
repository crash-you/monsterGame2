Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors 怪物生存者

An epic survival adventure where you battle endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving world. 一场史诗级的生存冒险，在不断进化的世界中与无尽的怪物浪潮战斗。

Game Overview 游戏概览

Monster Survivors is a thrilling survival game where you face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure. 怪物生存者是一款刺激的生存游戏，你将面对越来越困难的怪物波次。选择你的角色，升级你的能力，在这个充满挑战的冒险中尽可能地生存下去。

How to Play 游戏指南

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience to level up. Choose upgrades wisely to enhance your survival chances as the difficulty increases with time. 使用WASD或方向键移动你的角色。自动攻击附近的敌人并收集经验值来升级。随着时间推移难度会增加，请明智地选择升级项目以提高生存机会。

Game Features 游戏特色

Multiple Characters 多样角色

Choose from various characters with unique abilities and play styles. 从具有独特能力和游戏风格的多个角色中进行选择。

Upgrade System 升级系统

Level up and enhance your character's abilities as you progress. 随着游戏进程升级并增强你角色的能力。

Endless Challenges 无尽挑战

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival mode. 在无尽生存模式中面对越来越困难的怪物波次。

About Monster Survivors 关于怪物生存者

Monster Survivors draws inspiration from popular roguelike survival games, offering a unique twist with its monster-themed world. Your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible against endless waves of increasingly powerful monsters. 怪物生存者从流行的Roguelike生存游戏中汲取灵感，通过其怪物主题世界提供独特的游戏体验。你的目标简单但充满挑战：在越来越强大的无尽怪物浪潮中尽可能地生存。

As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience points to level up and enhance your character's abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely, as the right combination of skills can make the difference between survival and defeat. 当你击败敌人时，你将获得经验值来升级并增强角色的能力。明智地选择你的升级，因为正确的技能组合可能决定生存与失败的差异。

With its simple controls but deep strategic elements, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive in this monster-infested world? 凭借简单的控制但深层的战略元素，怪物生存者易于上手但难以精通。你能在这个充满怪物的世界中生存多久？
